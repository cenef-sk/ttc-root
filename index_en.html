Touch the culture

The "Touch the culture" project reflects on the current situation caused by Covid-19, but also on the long-term state of declining interest in some areas of the cultural and artistic environment. On this page you will find one of the intellectual outputs of the project, namely an application and a curatorial tool for making culture accessible to the general public.

Web Application

You don't want to install our app on your phone, play curated games right in your browser.

Mobile application

Download the app directly to your mobile device and enjoy curated games for you.

Curator

You are a curator and you want to make available or promote an exhibition or an art collection. With the help of our tool, you can easily prepare activities that will entertain, educate and motivate your supporters to physically visit your exhibition.
